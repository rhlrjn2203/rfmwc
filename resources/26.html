RFMW resource pages offer application specific design and product recommendations. We hope you find your answers here and are always available to help with your questions.

  RF Energy Resource
RFMW offers components and solutions for the RF Energy bands (433, 915MHz, 2.45GHz and 5.8GHz). We support customers with phase shifters, splitters, combiners, power amplifiers, circulators / isolators, terminations, 50 Ohm pallets, signal generators, power amplifier modules, cables and connectors.
     
  High Frequency Components
XMA offers Adapters, Attenuators, Terminations, Power Dividers and DC Blocks capable of performance at 40, 50 and 65GHz serving military, aerospace, test, measurement, medical and commercial markets globally.
     
  Cryogenic Components
XMA offers Attenuators and Terminations capable of performing at extremely low temperatures (4° Kelvin or -269.15° Celsius), with great stability.
     
  Low PIM Products
RFMW offers a variety of low PIM cable assemblies and adapters. Our suppliers understand the underlying causes of intermodulation distortion and ensure that correct materials and manufacturing techniques are used to minimize its effect.
     
  Test Components
Building a WiFi test bench? Let RFMW help you with Test & Measurement devices and components. Choose from Fixed Attenuators, DC Blocks, Adapters, Terminations, Directional Couplers, Power Dividers/Splitters, Cable Assemblies
     
  High Power RF Switches
Due to the growing interest in high power switch options, we've condensed our supplier's high power offering to a single, easy-to-view page including SPST switch elements to SPnT switches.
